# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want participation in Spliit Cloud to be a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Positive behavior: welcoming newcomers, assuming good faith, using inclusive language, giving and accepting constructive feedback, showing empathy.

Unacceptable: harassment, intimidation, discrimination, trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, personal attacks, publishing others' private information, sexualized language or imagery, sustained disruption.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to **conduct@spliit.cloud**. Reports are reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Consequences may include a written warning, temporary ban, or permanent ban depending on severity.
